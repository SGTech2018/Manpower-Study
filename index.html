Top 10 Skills in the IT Industry

The graph on the left is a stack diagram, listing the top 10 skills of a particular job. The color represents position level. The ranking is based on the weight of a skill, namely calculated by dividing total percentage (sum of junior, middle and senior) divided by the total count of jobs.

The graph shows the corresponding percentage of the level information: Junior (1 - 5 years working experience), Middle (5 - 10 years working experience), Senior (more than 10 years working experience).
